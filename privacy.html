SUtilBot - Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SUtilBot collects, uses, and protects any information that is collected while using the bot. By using SUtilBot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information Collection

SUtilBot may collect and store the following information from your server:

  • User IDs and Server IDs: These are stored for the purpose of moderation actions (warnings, kicks, etc.).
  • Warning Data: Information on the warnings issued to users.

3. How We Use the Data

The information collected by SUtilBot is used solely for the moderation and logging features of the bot. Specifically:

  • Moderation: To manage and track user infractions, such as warnings or kicks.
  • Auto-logging: The bot automatically logs moderation actions in the `mod-logs` channel.

4. Data Retention

User warning data is reset after 10 days if no further warnings are issued. Kicked users have their data removed from the system immediately after the action is executed.

5. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. Although we take precautions to safeguard the data, no system can guarantee absolute security.
